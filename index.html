Bagian 1

The four skills that are important for the success of your study are critical thinking, creative thinking, communication, and collaboration. Critical thinking is focused, careful analysis of something to better understand it. Creative thinking is the ability to come up with new and innovative ideas. Communication is the ability to convey information effectively and efficiently. Collaboration is the ability to work with others to achieve a common goal. These skills are vital to success in school and beyond




















Bagian 2

The four skills that are important for the success of your study are time management, organization, critical thinking, communication. Time management is crucial for success in your studies because it helps you prioritize your tasks and allocate your time effectively. By managing your time well, you can ensure that you have enough time to complete your assignments, study for exams, and participate in extracurricular activities.






















Bagian 3

Communication skills are important for success in your studies because they help you express your ideas clearly and effectively. By improving your communication skills, you can participate more effectively in class discussions, write better papers, and work more effectively in group projects.
